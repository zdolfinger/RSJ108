How To Create An Amazing Sports Article ...

Assembly time: 1 Hour 30 Minutes
Awesome rating: 85% awesome

Are you wondering how to an amazing article about Sports? It's super simple and very easy to implement your own designs and ideas. Time to try yourself! Follow the steps below and you'll create an amazing article in no time!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Computer / Writing Material
  • Audio Recording Equipment
  • An Open Mind
  • Eyes
  • Ears
  • Brain

  1. Picking your topic: Find an event you're interestd in.

    This will be the focus of your article. Make sure your passionate about the topic to ensure a great article.

    An event held in a stadium like this would serve as a great venue for your sports article.

  2. Documenting: It's important to take photos and document the event you're reporting.

    Remember, a good photo gives dimension to your article that word's just cannot convey.

    Be sure to adjust your camera settings before shooting to ensure good photo quality.

  3. Start your writing: Take notes and discuss with people around you to properly document the event.

    Be sure to write down the facts of the event and the names of the people you may get some quotes from.

    Write down things like the final score, contributing players, and notable plays in detail so you don't have to reference anything other than your own notes while writing.

  4. Final Edits: Once you have assembled your story with the facts and photographs you collected at the sporting event, you should begin to make the proper edits to your article.

    This is necessary to ensure your article is clear, consice, and tells the story the way you want it to be reflected.
